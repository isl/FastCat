HOW TO USE THE LEGAL ENTITIES SECTION

Inside this section you will find all legal entities used in the records that you have published to Fast Cat team. This is just a list of Legal Entities proposed by the system. The historian will decide whether two or more legal entities that appear in this list are the same or if two legal entities with the same information are different.  In order to do that follow those steps:

1.       Choose your template type in the filtering box in the top left corner and choose also the specific record. All legal entities used in this record will appear.

2.       If you see two legal entities in the list, and you are sure that those two lines refer to the same Legal entity, then go to the check box and choose all Legal Entities you want to merge. Then go to the top left corner and choose “Mark as same”. A pop up window will appear. For each field you can choose a preferred term. If you don’t want to choose one of the existing terms then you can add a new one. When you choose “Save changes” the Legal Entities that have been merged will have only one check box on their left side. The preferred term will appear in bold. If you added a preferred term it will appear in a separate row below.

3.       In order to undo the merging go to the gear button on the left and choose “Undo mark as same”

4.       In order to change the preferred term of the group or of a separate Legal Entity go to the gear button and choose “ Add/Remove preferred term”.

5.       If you go to the position info of an Legal Entity and you see that two appearances in different positions refer to another Legal Entity, then press to the button “ Mark as different” on the right side.  The two Legal Entities will appear in separate rows in the list.

Additional information:

·         The position info is dark orange when the Legal Entity is used in more than one different positions.

·         If one of the records that includes this Legal Entity has been published to the Research space the status next to the Legal Entity will change to “published”.

·         Inside this list all Legal Entities of the same template type that have identical identity information appear in a separate row.
